## My Portfolio ✨
Welcome to the official repository for my personal portfolio! 🚀

This portfolio is a showcase of my journey as a Computer Vision & AI Engineer, highlighting my skills, projects, and career milestones. It’s built using Next.js for a modern, dynamic experience and hosted seamlessly with Vercel for blazing-fast performance.

## 🌟 Features
- **About Me**: Learn about my professional background, career journey, and the motivation driving my work.
- **Skills and Expertise**: Explore my key areas of specialization, including Computer Vision, AI, and software development.
- **Projects**: A curated selection of impactful projects, complete with descriptions and insights into their significance.
- **Contact Me**: Get in touch via email.
